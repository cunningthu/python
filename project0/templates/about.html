Backstory

Raised in a vietnamese household with my mom, grandma, stepdad, older sister, younger half-sister, and younger half-brother. I had a good childhood, taken for granted until these days that seem so unfair. At the age of 12, my mom was divorced and we lived in a mobile home for the remainder of my high school days. I tell myself I'm not affected by it, tried to be brave and show that I could replace the role of a male figure in the family. I would be the one to fix the TVs, computers, leaks, and toys. This was also the time when I was getting exposed to my mom's financial issues.


Present Day

My vision is clear, my path is straight forward, and my mind is in the present moment. I've read a good amount of self help books to guide me through these past several months, where I was lost and feeling stressed from my mother's finacial burdens. In the most recent book, I learned not to be afraid of other humans, for we all want the same thing. I decided to relinquish this want for myself in order to live on stressless and free. So far, so good.
