What did I learn?

I spent a whole lot of money trying to complete all of these cosplay projects that I never finished one before starting another. It's not like I meant to give up on these, they just slowly became less exciting for me and I no longer wanted to work on them. Newer cooler characters would come to mind and I would start working on making costumes for them and once I was tired, or didn't want to work hard on making the cosplay, I put it to the side, swearing that I would not give up on them and one day, pick them back up.

It wasn't until my Marie Kondo came into my life, that I was able to declutter all my cosplay. I realized that seeing this unfinished/unloved work made me uphappy, and once I tossed it all, it was like clouds had cleared from my mind. This did not mean that I gave up on cosplay all together, I just started on a clean plate. I vowed to never start another cosplay without finishing dive current one. I am still working on my Hidomi Hibajiri.
